Schematics Checklist

1)Verify circuit block with datasheet or EVB
2)Verify that important signals have net labels
3)Verify with the project that it has a cover sheet with all the information
4)Verify that POWER rails are identified with relevant colors
5)Be sure to add relevant notes such as circulation current
6)Verify harnesses and connection ports between schematics (if applicable)
7)Apply ERC rules
8)Verify FOOTPRINT manager
9)Update parameters in sheet information
10)The schematic is in accordance with the hardware architecture
